The Best Show In The World:

Attack On Titan

Basic Description

Set in a world where humanity is forced to live in cities surrounded by three enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans; the story follows Eren Yeager, who vows to exterminate the Titans after they bring about the destruction of his hometown and the death of his mother

Source: Wikipedia

Why It's The Best Show

  • Every single moment no matter how small is always forsadowed (thats what makes the show really stand out from others)
  • Has every type of genre you could ask for
  • Incredibly unpredicatble and many plot twists that wont give you a moment to relax
  • shows the audience what war and sacrifice really means
